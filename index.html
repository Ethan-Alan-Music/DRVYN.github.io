About the Band

DRVYN consists of Burton, Ohio natives, Cody Charvat (Guitar), Donovan Springer (Bass / Vocals), Aaron Pawlak (Drums), Deano (Keys),and Ethan Alan (Vocals / Guitar). DRVYN is on a mission to rock the socks off of beings across the universe. DRVYN doesn't care about coloring inside the lines when it comes to their music. They are tired of being DRVYN by the strings of those in control. It's time to cut the strings and take back control! Come see us at a show near you! Follow us on all our social media platforms for updates and exciting content!
